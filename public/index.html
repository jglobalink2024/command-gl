The Problem

You built an AI team.
You became their coordinator.

Boutique operators running multi-agent stacks lose roughly a third of productive AI time manually relaying context between tools and tabs. COMMAND kills the relay and gives that time back.

01
No visibility
Agents are running, but you have no unified view of what they are doing, where they stalled, or which one needs your attention right now.
02
Broken handoffs
When work moves between agents, context collapses. You copy-paste between tabs and rebuild the state from scratch. Every single time.

Fit

Who COMMAND is for —
and who it is not.

We built this for operator-led firms that run real agent stacks — not for another generic “AI workspace” RFP.

COMMAND is for you if…
  • You run a boutique AI consulting or delivery firm (roughly solo to ~15 people).
  • You already use 3+ agents or tools and feel the pain of tab-hopping and manual relay.
  • You want one control plane for status, routing, and context — without hiring platform engineering.
COMMAND is probably not for you if…
  • You need a full enterprise RPA replacement or IT-owned automation program.
  • You already have a dedicated AI platform team owning observability end-to-end.
  • You are shopping for another undifferentiated “AI workspace” with no agent-ops focus.
