Why now · Why us
MCP (Model Context Protocol) and A2A (Agent-to-Agent) protocols standardized in 2025–2026 — the integration layer COMMAND sits above. Boutique firms began operationalizing agents in 2025. The coordination problem we solve didn't exist at scale before that.
