The Problem

You built an AI team.
You became their coordinator.

Boutique operators running multi-agent stacks lose a third of productive AI time manually relaying context between tools. COMMAND eliminates the relay.

01
No visibility
Agents are running. You have no unified view of what they’re doing, where they stalled, or which one needs your attention right now.
02
Broken handoffs
When work moves between agents, context collapses. You copy-paste between tabs and rebuild from scratch. Every single time.
03
No task routing
There’s no single place to assign a task to the right agent and track it to completion. Your stack runs on memory and habit.
04
You are the bridge
Your agents are powerful in isolation. But you’re connecting them manually — so your cognitive load grows with every agent you add.
